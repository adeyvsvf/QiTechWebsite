Our Story

Founded in 2015, Quest Innovative Tech Limited (QiTech) emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make clean, affordable solar energy accessible to every Nigerian home and business.

What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into Nigeria's premier solar energy company, serving hundreds of satisfied customers across the nation. Our journey has been driven by innovation, quality, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of Nigeria's renewable energy revolution, helping families and businesses reduce their carbon footprint while enjoying significant savings on electricity bills.
